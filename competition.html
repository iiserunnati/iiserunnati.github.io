Wires that connect, Mythbusters

“Wires that connect” and “Mythbusters” : A gentle dive into the sea of knowledge related to neuroscience, starting with the basics of how our brain evolved to the cellular units of the nervous system. After Dr. Vasudharani Devanathan’s illuminating talk, our friends Shivam and Lochan tackled some myths and busted them. “We use only 10% of our brain”, “Crossword puzzles improve memory” etc - are these facts or myths? A fun-filled interactive session of which we hope to conduct more.
