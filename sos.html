January 9th, 2021

Wires that connect, Mythbusters

“Wires that connect” and “Mythbusters” : A gentle dive into the sea of knowledge related to neuroscience, starting with the basics of how our brain evolved to the cellular units of the nervous system. After Dr. Vasudharani Devanathan’s illuminating talk, our friends Shivam and Lochan tackled some myths and busted them. “We use only 10% of our brain”, “Crossword puzzles improve memory” etc - are these facts or myths? A fun-filled interactive session of which we hope to conduct more.

July, 2020

Interactive session on viruses

An interactive session on viruses was conducted for the kids of SOS CV Tirupati, faculty kids and nearby school students by Dr, Vasudharani Devanathan and Dr. Pakala Suresh Babu.
The response from the children was very good with the kids actively asking questions and cleared their inquisitive questions about the current pandemic and other aspects.

Setting up a library at SOS

A book donation drive was held to start a library at SOS Children’s village, Tirupati. We received 233 story books from the institute.The children themselves helped out to sort, organize and document.
